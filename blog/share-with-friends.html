Share Amazon Cart With Friends

Have you ever found a bunch of cool stuff on Amazon and wanted to show it all to your friends at once? Maybe you’re planning a party, buying gifts, shopping for matching clothes, or just browsing for fun. It can be hard to send all the links one by one. But guess what? There’s an easier way.

You can share your whole Amazon cart with your friends using a free Chrome extension called Share Amazon Cart ↗. It makes a special link that shows everything in your cart. You can send this link to your friends by text, email, or on social media. They can click it and see all the items in one place.

Why Share Your Amazon Cart with Friends?

Shopping online is more fun with friends. Sharing your Amazon cart helps you:

  • Plan together - Perfect for birthday parties, holidays, road trips, or dorm room shopping.
  • Pick gifts - Share wish lists or gift ideas for birthdays, weddings, or baby showers.
  • Split orders - Group items to save on shipping or make one big order together.
  • Get opinions - Ask “Which one do you like better?” with pictures and prices all in one place.
  • Match outfits - If you and your besties want the same hoodie or shoes, you can all see the link easily.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about sharing a cart on Amazon with friends.

Can my friends change my cart?
No. They can only view it. If they want to buy something, they’ll add it to their own cart.
Do my friends need the extension too?
No. Only the person sharing the cart needs it. Your friends just open the link like normal.
Can I use it on mobile?
The extension only works on desktop (Chrome & Edge browsers), but the shared link works on any device—including phones.
Do I need to be logged in to share my Amazon cart?
No, you don't need to be logged into Amazon to make a shareable cart link. Additionally, your friends don’t need an account to view the link.
