Transfer Amazon Cart to Another Account

Transferring your Amazon cart can be a real time-saver, especially when you're collaborating with friends, family, or coworkers. Whether you're planning a group gift, comparing products, or just need a second opinion, transferring your cart ensures everyone is on the same page. Let's dive into how you can do this effortlessly.

Even though Amazon doesn't have a built-in way to transfer a cart, there are simple ways to do it. In this guide, you’ll learn how to transfer your Amazon cart, including how to use a Chrome Extension ↗ to make it fast and easy.

Why Transfer Your Amazon Cart?

Imagine you've spent hours picking out the perfect items for a project or gift. Now, you want someone else to review your choices or help finalize the purchase. Instead of manually listing each item or taking screenshots, transferring your cart provides a seamless solution. It's efficient, straightforward, and keeps everyone in the loop.

Can You Transfer Your Cart on Amazon?

Yes! You can either:

  • Use the extension Share Amazon Cart ↗
  • Move items to a wishlist and share that
  • Copy and paste links to individual items

The Best Way: Use the "Share Amazon Cart" Extension

The best way to share your cart is to use the Share Amazon Cart Chrome Extension ↗. This tool lets you turn your cart into a simple link that you can send to anyone.

What Is the Share Amazon Cart Extension?

This is a free tool made for Chrome / Edge users. It creates a link that includes all the items in your cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see the same items and can add them to their own cart. It works across different Amazon regions and doesn’t need your Amazon login or password.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the Share Amazon Cart extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add items to your Amazon cart like you normally would.
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any Amazon page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • You don't need to manage a wishlist or copy every product link.
  • Works with Amazon.com, international sites, Amazon Fresh, and Whole Foods.
  • Transfers the exact items and quantities.
  • Transfers the exact colors and sizes.
  • The recipient gets all items with one click.

Manual Process

  1. Open each item in the browser.
  2. Sign in to the new account.
  3. Click 'Add To Cart' on each one.

Note: When the recipient clicks 'Add To Cart', it won't clear the contents of their existing cart. So anything there previously will remain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I transfer my cart without being signed into my Amazon account?
Yes, without signing in, you can still transfer your Amazon cart with the extension.
Is there a limit to the number of items I can transfer to another account?
You can transfer as many items as you like to another account. In some cases, Amazon may limit the total quantity that can be purchased for certain items.
Can I transfer my Amazon Fresh cart too?
Yes! If you add items to your Amazon Fresh or Whole Foods cart, the extension can transfer the entire cart to another account. It works with most grocery items, as long as they’re in your Amazon shopping cart.
