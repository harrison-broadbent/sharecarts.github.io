How To Share Amazon Cart Without Signing In

There are many reasons to share your Amazon cart without signing in. It's common to browse Amazon from a work computer or other public places (like a library).

Even though Amazon doesn't have a built-in way to share your cart, there are simple ways to do it. In this guide, you’ll learn how to share your Amazon cart, including how to use a Chrome Extension ↗ to make it fast and easy.

Why Share Your Amazon Cart Without Signing In?

It’s an efficient, secure way to share your shopping list without worrying about exposing personal information on a work, shared, or public device. This method keeps everything simple and safe, especially when you're on the go or using a computer that isn't yours.

Can You Share Your Cart on Amazon Without Signing In?

Yes! You can either:

  • Use the extension Share Amazon Cart ↗
  • Copy and paste links to individual items

You can't add Amazon items to an Amazon wishlist without signing in.

The Best Way: Use the "Share Amazon Cart" Extension

The best way to share your cart is to use the Share Amazon Cart Chrome Extension ↗. This tool lets you turn your cart into a simple link that you can send to anyone.

What Is the Share Amazon Cart Extension?

This is a free tool made for Chrome / Edge users. It creates a link that includes all the items in your cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see the same items and can add them to their own cart. It works across different Amazon regions and doesn’t need your Amazon login or password.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the Share Amazon Cart extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add items to your Amazon cart like you normally would (you don't need to be logged in).
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any Amazon page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • You don't need to copy every product link.
  • Works with Amazon.com, international sites, Amazon Fresh, and Whole Foods.
  • You can share your Amazon cart without an account.
  • Shares the exact items and quantities.
  • The recipient gets all items with one click.

Manual Process

  1. Open each item in the browser.
  2. Copy the URL from your browser.
  3. Paste each link into a message.

This works if you only have a few products. It’s not efficient for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.

Can I share my cart without an Amazon account?
Yes, you can share your Amazon cart without an account. Send your cart to someone who does, like friends or family.
Can I transfer my cart to another account?
Yes, these same steps can be used to Send Amazon Cart or Transfer Amazon Cart to another account.
Does my shared cart link stay updated or synced?
No, the link reflects your cart items at the time it was created. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the extension from this guide to create a new link.
