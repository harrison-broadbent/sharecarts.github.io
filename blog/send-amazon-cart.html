Send Amazon Cart to Someone

Amazon is a massive online marketplace, and millions of people shop there every day. But what if you want to send your cart to someone?

Even though Amazon doesn't have a built-in way to send your cart, there are simple ways to do it. In this guide, you’ll learn how to send your Amazon cart, including how to use a Chrome Extension ↗ to make it fast and easy.

Why Send Your Amazon Cart?

One of the biggest reasons people send their Amazon carts is to get feedback before making a purchase. Sometimes, you may be unsure about the items you’ve added. Sharing your cart with friends or family can give you the chance to ask if the products are worth buying or if there might be better options. This helps make sure you’re making the right choice before spending your money.

Another reason is when you need to collaborate with others. Let’s say you and a group of friends are buying a gift together. Rather than each person trying to pick something from different websites, you can just share your Amazon cart and agree on which items to buy. It’s a simple way to make sure everyone is on the same page.

Can You Send Your Cart on Amazon?

Yes! You can either:

  • Use the extension Share Amazon Cart ↗
  • Move items to a wishlist and send that
  • Copy and paste links to individual items

The Best Way: Use the "Share Amazon Cart" Extension

The best way to send your cart is to use the Share Amazon Cart Chrome Extension ↗. This tool lets you turn your cart into a simple link that you can send to anyone.

What Is the Share Amazon Cart Extension?

This is a free tool made for Chrome / Edge users. It creates a link that includes all the items in your cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see the same items and can add them to their own cart. It works across different Amazon regions and doesn’t need your Amazon login or password.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the Share Amazon Cart extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add items to your Amazon cart like you normally would.
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any Amazon page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • You don't need to manage a wishlist or copy every product link.
  • Works with Amazon.com, international sites, Amazon Fresh, and Whole Foods.
  • Sends the exact items and quantities.
  • Sends the exact colors and sizes.
  • The recipient gets all items with one click.

Manual Process

  1. Open each item in the browser.
  2. Copy the URL from your browser.
  3. Paste each link into a message.

This works if you only have a few products. It’s not efficient for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.
