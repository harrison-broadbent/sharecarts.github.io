Share Amazon Cart With Co-Workers

If you work in an office, school, or team environment, you probably need to buy things together sometimes. It might be supplies, snacks, decorations, or even gifts. Instead of sending a long list of links to your co-workers, there’s a much easier way: share your Amazon cart using a free browser extension.

The tool is called Share Amazon Cart ↗, and it lets you create a simple link to your Amazon cart. You can send that link to your co-workers, and they can see exactly what’s in your cart. It’s fast, simple, and helpful.

Why Share Your Amazon Cart with Co-Workers?

Sharing your Amazon cart with co-workers helps everyone stay on the same page. Whether you're planning a party, organizing supplies, or putting together a group order, a shared cart makes it easy for everyone to see what’s being bought.

Here are a few real-world examples:

  • Office Supplies - Pens, sticky notes, printer paper, folders—it's easier to buy everything at once.
  • Event Planning - Planning a holiday party or birthday celebration? Share a cart with decorations, snacks, and gifts.
  • Team Gifts - Buying a farewell gift or birthday present for a co-worker? Let everyone see the ideas and pitch in.
  • Shared Projects - Need tools or parts for a work project? Add them to a cart and get input from the team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about sharing a cart on Amazon with co-workers.

Can I update the cart after sharing the link?
Yes, but you’ll need to generate a new link and share it again.
Do co-workers need the extension?
No. Only you need the extension to create the link. They can open the link in any browser.
Can co-workers add to my cart?
No. They can only view it. If your co-workers want to buy something, they’ll add it to their own cart.
