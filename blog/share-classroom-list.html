Share a Classroom List With Your Students

Sometimes, teachers want to share a list of things with their students. It could be school supplies, books, or even gifts. One easy way to do this is by using Amazon. But Amazon does not make it easy to share your cart directly. Luckily, there is a simple tool that can help: Share Amazon Cart ↗. This tool makes it fast and easy to show your Amazon shopping cart to your students.

Why Share an Amazon Cart with Students?

There are many reasons teachers might want to do this:

  • Class Supplies - You can show students the exact notebooks, pens, or folders they need.
  • Books for Reading - Maybe your class is reading a new book. You can add it to your cart and share it with everyone.
  • Project Materials - If students are doing a science or art project, you can send them a cart with all the materials.
  • Gifts - If students are doing a classroom gift exchange, you can create a list of approved or suggested gifts.

It’s also helpful for parents. If they want to help out, they can use the shared cart to buy items and send them to the classroom.
