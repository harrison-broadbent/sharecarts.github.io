How to Share Walmart Cart

Walmart is one of the biggest online stores in the United States. You can buy groceries, clothes, electronics, toys, and many other things from Walmart.com ↗. But what if you want to shop with a friend or family member? What if you both want to share a cart so you can view the same items? That’s where sharing your Walmart cart becomes useful.

In this guide, you’ll learn how to share a Walmart cart, why it’s helpful, and the easiest way to do it using a free tool ↗. Don’t worry—it's simple and only takes a few seconds to get started.

Why Share Your Walmart Cart?

Here are some common reasons people like to share their Walmart carts:

  • Grocery Planning Together - Families can build a weekly grocery list together. Everyone can add what they need.
  • Split Costs with Roommates - Living with others? Add shared household items and split the bill.
  • Group Gifts or Events - Planning a party, wedding, or baby shower? Share a cart with the group so everyone can pitch in.
  • Helping Someone Shop - If your parents or grandparents don’t shop online, you can build their cart for them and send it over.
  • Surprise Shopping Made Easy - Want your partner to pick their own birthday gift? Share a cart of ideas!

Can You Share Your Walmart Cart?

Yes! Even though Walmart doesn’t have a built-in “share cart” feature, it is still possible.

The best way to do it is by using a Chrome extension called Share Amazon Cart ↗. This tool helps you turn your cart into a link that you can share with anyone. There are also other ways like using wishlists or copying product links, but the Chrome extension is the most straightforward way on Walmart.

Using the “Share Amazon Cart” Chrome Extension

What It Is

The Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗ is a free tool for Chrome and Edge users. It lets you create a link from your Walmart cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see all the items and can add them to their own cart with one click.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add items to your Walmart cart like you normally would.
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any Walmart page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

The recipient opens the link and adds the same items to their cart.

Note: It does not clear anything previously in their cart.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • Share a cart without having to share accounts
  • Works with Walmart.com, Walmart Grocery, and Walmart Business.
  • Shares exact items and quantities
  • Shares exact brands and sizes

  • Copy the URL from your browser.
  • Paste each link into a message.

This works if you only have a few products. It’s not ideal for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about sharing a cart on Walmart. Have a different question and can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out here and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Can I share my Walmart cart with someone?
Yes, you can share your Walmart cart with anyone you want. This includes your friends, family, roommates, co-workers, or even with parents and students.
On which sites can I share a Walmart cart?
Walmart.com ↗ (USA) and Walmart Business ↗ are supported for cart sharing.
Does my shared cart link stay updated or synced?
No, the link reflects your cart items at the time it was created. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the extension ↗ from this guide to create a new link.
Do I need to be signed into my Walmart account?
No, without signing in, you can share or receive a cart.
Is an extension required to receive the cart?
No, you do not need an extension to receive a Walmart cart—only to share one.
Can I send my cart to another account?
Yes, these same steps can be used to send Walmart cart or transfer Walmart cart to another account.
Can I share my whole Walmart cart?
There is no limit to how many total items you can send using the extension. Walmart may limit the total quantity that can be purchased for certain items.
Will the cart links I create expire?
No, the links stay active indefinitely and are accessible on all desktop and mobile browsers. Product availability may vary.
Will the other person see my address or payment info?
No! The cart link only shows the item names and prices. Your name, address, and credit card info stay private.
Can I share my Amazon cart?
Yes, in addition to Walmart, you can share your cart on other top online stores. Learn how to share your Amazon cart with our in-depth guide.

Conclusion

Sharing your Walmart cart makes online shopping easier and more fun. Whether you’re planning meals with your family, organizing a group gift, or just want a second opinion before buying something, a shared cart saves time and effort.

The Chrome extension ↗ is the easiest and fastest way to do it. It takes just a few clicks and saves you a lot of time. If you don’t want to use an extension, wishlists and copy-pasting links also work, though they take more effort.
