How to Share Walgreens Cart

Walgreens is one of the most popular places to shop online for health, wellness, and everyday items. From cold medicine to shampoo, snacks to skincare, Walgreens has just about everything you might need. Shopping from their website is quick and convenient. You don’t have to leave your house, and everything gets delivered right to your door.

Let’s say you’re buying items for your whole family. Or maybe you’re putting together a care package for a friend. Maybe you're organizing a school supply run with other parents. In any of these situations, it helps to show someone else what’s in your cart. You want to make sure everyone agrees on what’s being bought. You want them to check the brands, sizes, or types of products. And sometimes, you just want a second opinion before you click “place order.”

In this guide, you’ll learn how to share a Walgreens cart, why it’s helpful, and the easiest way to do it using a free tool ↗. Don’t worry—it's simple and only takes a few seconds to get started.

Why Share Your Walgreens Cart?

There are lots of reasons to share your Walgreens cart with someone:

  • Family Health Shopping - You’re ordering cough syrup, tissues, vitamins, and first aid stuff. You want your spouse or parent to double-check before you hit "checkout."
  • Restocking Home Essentials - Need to grab hand soap, toothpaste, deodorant, and snacks? Share your cart with your roommate or partner so they can add what they need too.
  • Gift Shopping - It’s Mother’s Day or Christmas, and you want to build a custom gift set with lotion, candles, and makeup. Send your cart to your sibling and work on it together.
  • Helping Someone Sick or Busy - You’re buying supplies for someone who’s unwell or super busy. Let them see what you’re ordering so they can approve it or ask for changes.
  • Teacher/Classroom Supplies - Teachers and parents often shop for health items, wipes, tissues, and snacks. If you’re helping out, it’s easier to share the cart and avoid repeat orders.

Can You Share Your Walgreens Cart?

Yes! Even though Walgreens doesn’t have a built-in “share cart” feature, it is still possible.

The best way to do it is by using a Chrome extension called Share Amazon Cart ↗. This tool helps you turn your cart into a link that you can share with anyone. There are also other ways like using shopping lists or copying product links, but the Chrome extension is the most straightforward way on Walgreens.

Using the “Share Amazon Cart” Chrome Extension

What It Is

The Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗ is a free tool for Chrome and Edge users. It lets you create a link from your Walgreens cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see all the items and can add them to their own cart with one click.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add grocery items to your Walgreens cart like you normally would.
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any Walgreens page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

The recipient opens the link and adds the same items to their cart.

Note: It does not clear anything previously in their cart.

Manual Process

  • Open each item in the browser.
  • Copy the URL from your browser.
  • Paste each link into a message.

This works if you only have a few products. It’s not ideal for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about sharing a cart on Walgreens. Have a different question and can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out here and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Can I share my Walgreens cart with someone?
Yes, you can share your Walgreens cart with anyone you want. This includes your friends, family, roommates, co-workers, or even with parents and students.
Does my shared cart link stay updated or synced?
No, the link reflects your cart items at the time it was created. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the extension ↗ from this guide to create a new link.
Do I need to be signed into my Walgreens account?
No, without signing in, you can share or receive a cart.
Is an extension required to receive the cart?
No, you do not need an extension to receive a Walgreens cart—only to share one.
Can I send my cart to another account?
Yes, these same steps can be used to send Walgreens cart or transfer Walgreens cart to another account.
Can I share my whole Walgreens cart?
There is no limit to how many total items you can send using the extension. Walgreens may limit the total quantity that can be purchased for certain items.
Will the cart links I create expire?
No, the links stay active indefinitely and are accessible on all desktop and mobile browsers. Product availability may vary.
Will the other person see my address or payment info?
No! The cart link only shows the item names and prices. Your name, address, and credit card info stay private.

Conclusion

Sharing your Walgreens cart makes planning easier, saves time, and helps avoid mistakes. Whether you’re shopping for home health, family gifts, or daily essentials, you’ll feel way more in control when everyone sees the same cart.

The Chrome extension ↗ is the easiest and fastest way to do it. It takes just a few clicks and saves you a lot of time. If you don’t want to use an extension, shopping lists and copy-pasting links also work, though they take more effort.
