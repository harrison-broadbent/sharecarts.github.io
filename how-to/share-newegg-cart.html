How to Share Newegg Cart

If you’re into computers, gaming, or tech in general, Newegg is probably one of your favorite stores. It’s a popular website where you can buy everything from graphics cards to keyboards, RAM, SSDs, motherboards, and full PC setups. You can even put together your own computer using parts from Newegg.

But what if you want to share your Newegg cart with a friend? Maybe you're building a custom gaming PC and want someone to check it. Or maybe you're helping someone else choose the right parts. That’s when you need a way to share your Newegg build or cart.

In this guide, you’ll learn how to share a Newegg cart, why it’s helpful, and the easiest way to do it using a free tool ↗. Don’t worry—it's simple and only takes a few seconds to get started.

Why Share Your Newegg Cart?

People share Newegg carts for lots of reasons. Here are a few common ones:

  • Building a PC with friends - Let’s say you’re helping someone build their first gaming PC. You can add the best parts to your cart and share the link.
  • Double-checking compatibility - Not sure if your CPU and motherboard go together? Share your cart with a tech-savvy friend to make sure.
  • Group tech buys - Maybe your office or gaming squad wants to buy similar parts together. Everyone can see the cart and pitch in.
  • Getting advice - You found some deals on SSDs and GPUs. But you want to make sure you’re not missing something. Share your cart and ask, “What do you think?”
  • Making YouTube or blog content - Some creators like to post links to their Newegg builds so fans can copy or buy the same setup.

Can You Share Your Newegg Cart?

Yes! Even though Newegg doesn’t have a built-in “share cart” feature, it is still possible.

The best way to do it is by using a Chrome extension called Share Amazon Cart ↗. This tool helps you turn your cart into a link that you can share with anyone. There are also other ways like using wishlists or copying product links, but the Chrome extension is the most straightforward way on Newegg.

Using the “Share Amazon Cart” Chrome Extension

What It Is

The Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗ is a free tool for Chrome and Edge users. It lets you create a link from your Newegg cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see all the items and can add them to their own cart with one click.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add items to your Newegg cart like you normally would.
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any Newegg page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

The recipient opens the link and adds the same items to their cart.

Note: It does not clear anything previously in their cart.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • Works with Newegg.com, Newegg Canada, and Newegg Business.
  • Shares exact items and quantities
  • Shares exact brands and options

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about sharing a cart on Newegg. Have a different question and can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out here and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Can I share my Newegg cart with someone?
Yes, you can share your Newegg cart with anyone you want. This includes your friends, family, roommates, co-workers, or even with parents and students.
On which sites can I share a Newegg cart?
Newegg.com ↗ (USA), Newegg.ca ↗ (Canada), and Newegg Business ↗ are supported for cart sharing.
Does my shared cart link stay updated or synced?
No, the link reflects your cart items at the time it was created. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the extension ↗ from this guide to create a new link.
Do I need to be signed into my Newegg account?
No, without signing in, you can share or receive a cart.
Is an extension required to receive the cart?
No, you do not need an extension to receive a Newegg cart—only to share one.
Can I send my cart to another account?
Yes, these same steps can be used to send Newegg cart or transfer Newegg cart to another account.
Can I share my whole Newegg cart?
There is no limit to how many total items you can send using the extension. Newegg may limit the total quantity that can be purchased for certain items.
Will the cart links I create expire?
No, the links stay active indefinitely and are accessible on all desktop and mobile browsers. Product availability may vary.
Will the other person see my address or payment info?
No! The cart link only shows the item names and prices. Your name, address, and credit card info stay private.

Thanks to a Chrome extension ↗, you can now share your Newegg build or shopping cart with just one click. It’s fast, free, and works super quickly. Whether you’re building your dream PC or helping a friend upgrade their setup, this tool saves time and hassle.
