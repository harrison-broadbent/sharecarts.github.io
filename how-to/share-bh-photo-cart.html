How to Share B&H Photo Cart

B&H Photo is a popular store for photographers, videographers, tech lovers, and creators. It sells cameras, computers, drones, lighting gear, microphones, and all kinds of electronics. Whether you're a pro filmmaker or just someone looking for a good webcam, B&H has what you need.

If you’ve ever shopped on their website, you know how easy it is to build a cart. You find what you want, click “Add to Cart,” and boom—it’s saved for checkout. But what if you want to share your B&H cart with someone else?

In this guide, you’ll learn how to share a B&H Photo cart, why it’s helpful, and the easiest way to do it using a free tool ↗. Don’t worry—it's simple and only takes a few seconds to get started.

Why Share Your B&H Photo Cart?

There are tons of reasons why someone would want to share a cart on B&H Photo:

  • Photographers or videographers picking out gear for a shoot and sending the list to a producer.
  • Freelancers showing clients a setup before ordering.
  • IT teams building a list of office tech to get manager approval.
  • Friends shopping together for podcast equipment or studio builds.
  • Students putting together gear for school and getting feedback from teachers or parents.

Can You Share Your B&H Photo Cart?

Yes! Even though B&H Photo doesn’t have a built-in “share cart” feature, it is still possible.

The best way to do it is by using a Chrome extension called Share Amazon Cart ↗. This tool helps you turn your cart into a link that you can share with anyone. There are also other ways like using lists or copying product links, but the Chrome extension is the most straightforward way on B&H Photo.

Using the “Share Amazon Cart” Chrome Extension

What It Is

The Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗ is a free tool for Chrome and Edge users. It lets you create a link from your B&H Photo cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see all the items and can add them to their own cart with one click.

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add items to your B&H Photo cart like you normally would.
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any B&H Photo page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

The recipient opens the link and adds the same items to their cart.

Note: It does not clear anything previously in their cart.

Manual Process

  • Open each item in the browser.
  • Copy the URL from your browser.
  • Paste each link into a message.

This works if you only have a few products. It’s not ideal for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about sharing a cart on B&H Photo. Have a different question and can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out here and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Can I share my B&H Photo cart with someone?
Yes, you can share your B&H Photo cart with anyone you want. This includes your friends, family, roommates, co-workers, or even with parents and students.
Does my shared cart link stay updated or synced?
No, the link reflects your cart items at the time it was created. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the extension ↗ from this guide to create a new link.
Do I need to be signed into my B&H Photo account?
No, without signing in, you can share or receive a cart.
Is an extension required to receive the cart?
No, you do not need an extension to receive a B&H Photo cart—only to share one.
Can I send my cart to another account?
Yes, these same steps can be used to send B&H Photo cart or transfer B&H Photo cart to another account.
Can I share my whole B&H Photo cart?
There is no limit to how many total items you can send using the extension. B&H Photo may limit the total quantity that can be purchased for certain items.
Will the cart links I create expire?
No, the links stay active indefinitely and are accessible on all desktop and mobile browsers. Product availability may vary.
Will the other person see my address or payment info?
No! The cart link only shows the item names and prices. Your name, address, and credit card info stay private.
