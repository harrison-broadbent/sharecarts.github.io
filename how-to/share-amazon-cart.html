How to Share Amazon Cart

Amazon is the world's largest online retailer. You can buy almost anything—clothes, electronics, books, groceries, and more. But what if you want to show someone else what you’re planning to buy? Maybe you're shopping with a friend, planning a gift with your partner, or asking family members to view your cart. That’s where Amazon cart sharing comes in.

Even though Amazon doesn't have a built-in way to share your shopping cart, there are simple ways to do it. In this guide, you’ll learn how to share your Amazon cart, including how to use a Chrome Extension ↗ to make it fast and easy.

Why Share Your Amazon Cart?

Sharing your Amazon cart makes shopping with others much easier. It’s useful when you’re buying gifts for friends or planning a big family order. Imagine coordinating a birthday present, a holiday shopping list, or even stocking up on household items with multiple people. Instead of sending many separate links, you can share one cart. This way, everyone sees the same items, can suggest changes, or copy the cart if they want to buy the same things.

It saves time and reduces mistakes, like forgetting something important or accidentally duplicating items. Sharing your cart also helps with organizing group orders at work or planning a special event, making the process smoother and more efficient.

Here are some common reasons people like to share their Amazon carts:

  • Stay Organized - Everyone sees the same shopping list. Whether you’re planning a party or group gift, it helps keep things in one place. No one gets left out or confused about what’s already picked.
  • Perfect for Groups - Families, friends, coworkers, classmates—everyone benefits from shared carts. It works great for planning holidays, birthdays, office events, or even just sharing fun finds.
  • Split Costs Easily - Everyone sees the prices and totals. That makes it easy to divide up the bill or take turns covering items. You can even decide who buys what based on the cart.
  • Great for Any Occasion - Use it for school shopping, weddings, potlucks, holidays, or even vacations. If multiple people are involved, sharing your cart just makes sense.
  • Safe and Private - The people you share with won’t see your Amazon login, payment info, or saved addresses. Only the cart items are shared—nothing else.

The Best Way: Use "Share Amazon Cart" Extension

The best way to share your cart is to use the Share Amazon Cart Chrome Extension ↗. This tool lets you turn your cart into a simple link that you can send to anyone.

What Is the Share Amazon Cart Extension?

This is a free tool made for Chrome / Edge users. It creates a link that includes all the items in your cart. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see the same items and can add them to their own cart. It works across different Amazon regions and doesn’t need your Amazon login or password. It is trusted by thousands of shoppers worldwide!

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the Share Amazon Cart extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add items to your Amazon cart like you normally would.
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any Amazon page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

The recipient opens the link and adds the same items to their cart.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • You don't need to manage a wishlist or copy every product link.
  • Works with Amazon.com, international sites, Amazon Fresh, and Whole Foods.
  • Shares the exact items and quantities.
  • Shares the exact colors and sizes.
  • The recipient gets all items with one click.

Another Option: Create and Share Wishlist

Here are the steps to create and share an Amazon Wishlist:

  1. Create a wishlist
    1. Hover over "Accounts & Lists" in the top right corner.
    2. Click "Your Lists".
    3. Select "Create a List".
  2. Add Items
    1. Open the product page.
    2. Click "Add to List" near the "Add to Cart" button.
    3. Pick your Wishlist from the drop-down menu.
  3. Share Your Wishlist
    1. Go back to "Your Lists".
    2. Select the list you want to share.
    3. Click the "Send list to others" or "Invite" button.

Wishlist Drawbacks

If you’re hoping someone will just buy everything in one go—like for a group gift or a bulk order—it’s not the smoothest option. The person has to go through the list and manually add each item to their own cart, which can be a bit of a hassle. Plus, details like size, color, or quantity might not get applied to the recipient cart.

Additionally, you must be signed into Amazon to create and share a wishlist. Given all these limitations, we highly recommend using the option mentioned above.

Manual Process

  1. Open each item in the browser.
  2. Copy the URL from your browser.
  3. Paste each link into a message.

This works if you only have a few products. It’s not efficient for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I share my Amazon cart with someone?
Yes, you can share your Amazon cart with anyone you want. This includes your friends, family, roommates, co-workers, or even with parents and students.
Can I share my Amazon Fresh cart?
Yes, in addition to the main Amazon website, carts on Amazon Fresh and Whole Foods can be shared.
Does my shared cart link stay updated or synced?
No, the link reflects your cart items at the time it was created. If you change the items in your cart, you should use the extension ↗ from this guide to create a new link.
Where can I share an Amazon cart?
Amazon operates across 5 continents with around 20 countries having dedicated websites for shoppers and sellers. All the steps and examples in this guide apply to every Amazon website worldwide, including: https://amazon.com ↗, https://www.amazon.ca ↗, https://www.amazon.fr ↗, https://www.amazon.de ↗, https://www.amazon.it ↗, https://www.amazon.co.uk ↗.

Some regional restrictions or differences in product availability may affect what the recipient can see or purchase.

Can I share my whole Amazon cart?
Yes, you can share your entire Amazon cart. There is no limit to how many different items you can send using the extension. In some cases, Amazon may limit the total quantity that can be purchased for certain items.

Does it clear the other persons cart?
No, it does not clear anything previously in their cart. It only adds the items you share.
Do I need to be signed into my Amazon account?
No, without signing in, you can share or receive a cart.
Is an extension required to receive the cart?
No, you do not need an extension to receive an Amazon cart—only to share one.
Can I transfer my cart to another account?
Yes, these same steps can be used to send Amazon cart or transfer Amazon cart to another account.
Will the cart links I create expire?
No, your cart links won’t expire—they stay active for as long as you need. You can open them on any browser, whether you're on a computer or using your iPhone. Just keep in mind that product availability might change.
Can I share my Amazon Haul cart?
Yes, anything in your Amazon Haul cart can be shared. All the same steps apply for the newly launched Amazon Haul storefront.
Can I share my Amazon Business cart?
Yes, your Amazon Business cart can be used for team purchases. The person in charge of buying can review, approve, and place the order.
Can I share my Amazon cart if I use Amazon Household?
Yes, if you share Prime benefits through Amazon Household, you can still share a cart with another person. The same steps in this guide still apply.
Can I share my Walmart cart?
Yes, in addition to Amazon, you can share your cart on other top online stores. Learn how to share your Walmart cart with our in-depth guide.
