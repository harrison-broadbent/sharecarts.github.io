How to Share Amazon Haul Cart

Amazon Haul is Amazon’s new budget section that launched in late 2024, and honestly, it’s kind of a big deal. If you love scrolling for cheap finds, getting good stuff under $10, and scoring trendy items fast, this is exactly your vibe. You’ll find it at amazon.com/haul/store. Just type "Haul" in the search bar and boom—you’re in.

So what’s it all about? Think of it as Amazon’s answer to Shein, Temu, and other fast-deal platforms. The stuff here is cheap (like, really cheap), quick to browse, and always changing. It’s packed with little must-haves—things you didn’t even know you wanted until they popped up in your feed.

In this guide, we’ll show you how to share your Amazon Haul cart. We’ll also walk through using the Share Amazon Cart Chrome extension ↗, which is the easiest way to share your Amazon Haul items.

Why Share Your Amazon Haul Cart?

One of the biggest reasons to share your Amazon Haul cart is that it saves others a ton of time. Amazon offers millions of products, and searching through them all can be overwhelming. By sharing your cart, you’re doing the hard work for others. They don’t have to go through the endless options, read hundreds of reviews, or compare prices across dozens of listings. Instead, they can click on the link to your cart and immediately access a curated collection of products that you’ve already researched and selected. For example, if you’ve found a great set of kitchen tools or the perfect pair of clothing, sharing your cart lets others skip the hassle of finding those items themselves.

Can You Share Your Amazon Haul Cart?

Yes! Even though Amazon doesn’t have a built-in “share cart” feature, it is still possible.

The best way to do it is by using a Chrome extension called Share Amazon Cart ↗. This tool helps you turn your cart into a link that you can share with anyone. There are also other ways like using wishlists or copying product links, but the Chrome extension is the most straightforward way on Amazon Haul.

Using the “Share Amazon Cart” Chrome Extension

What It Is

The Share Amazon Cart Extension ↗ is a free tool for Chrome and Edge users. It lets you create a link from your Amazon cart. This link includes everything in your cart—even Amazon Haul items. When someone clicks the link, they’ll see all the items and can add them to their own cart with one click. It is trusted by thousands of shoppers worldwide!

The extension requires minimal permissions and installs quickly.

Why It Works for Amazon Haul

Amazon Haul is part of the main Amazon website. So when you add items to your Haul cart, they appear in your normal cart too. That’s why this extension works—it reads your cart and builds a shareable link to it.

How to Use the Extension

  1. Install the extension. Go to the Share Amazon Cart extension page ↗ and install it on Chrome or Edge.
  2. Shop like usual. Add items to your Amazon cart like you normally would.
  3. Click "Share Cart". Open any Amazon page and click the new “Share Cart” button.
  4. Copy & Share. Copy the shareable link and send it to someone.

The recipient opens the link and adds the same items to their cart.

Note: It does not clear anything previously in their cart.

Why It’s Better Than Other Methods

  • You don't need to manage a wishlist or copy every product link.
  • Works with Amazon.com, international sites, Amazon Fresh, and Whole Foods.
  • Shares the exact items and quantities.
  • Shares the exact brands and sizes.
  • The recipient gets all items with one click.

Manual Process

  • Open each item in the browser.
  • Copy the URL from your browser.
  • Paste each link into a message.

This works if you only have a few products. It’s not ideal for large or complex carts. You risk sending the wrong link or missing an item, which can cause confusion for the people you’re sharing with.

Yes, the extension will combine your regular Amazon cart and Haul cart into a single link.

Amazon Haul prioritizes ultra-low pricing, has longer shipping times, and uses a separate checkout.
